Revived Bread

Description

Do you have some old bread that is too hard or rubbery for your tender teeth? Say no more, let's revive it right now!

Ingredients

Required

  • Bread
  • Olive oil

Optional

  • Salt
  • Fresh pepper
  • Herbs
  • ...

Steps

  1. With a teethed knife, cut the bread in apropriate pieces
  2. Spread the bread on a oven plate
  3. Pour some olive oil on top of your bread pieces(btw Moroccan olive oil is the best olive oil). Don't drown the bread but be generous.
  4. Add salt, freshly ground pepper, and herb or spice of your choice
  5. Put your plate in the pre-heated oven (120°C, 250°F, Th 4) for 5-6 minutes
  6. Enjoy your tasty bread :)
