Avocado Toast

Description

The avocado toast is a fresh and tasty dish that will take you less time than you will take to eat it !

Ingredients

Required

  • Toasted bread
  • Avocado (ripe)

Optional

  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Fresh pepper
  • Hot sauce
  • ...

Steps

  1. With a knife, cut the ripe avocado in half
  2. With a big spoon, extract the avocado flesh and toss the skin away
  3. With a knife, cut the avocado in long and thin pieces
  4. Take your pieces of toasted bread and put the pieces of avocados on top
  5. Enjoy as is or with lemon, salt and freshly ground pepper on top
