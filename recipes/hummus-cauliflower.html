Hummus With Cauliflower

Description

Hummus is tasty and nutritious. Cauliflower is tasty and nutritious. Hummus with Caulifolower is double tasty and nutritious.

Ingredients

Required

  • Hummus (homemade or organic from supermarket
  • Cauliflower (fresh)

Optional

  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Fresh pepper
  • Hot sauce
  • Pomegrenate
  • Pine nuts
  • Basilic
  • ...

Steps

  1. Make your hummus (chickpeas, garlic, olive oil, tahini, all in the blender), or just buy hummus
  2. With a knife, cut the cauliflower in pieces no bigger than the size of your thumb (careful not to cut your thumb though)
  3. Boil some water in a pan
  4. Put the cauliflower in the water
  5. Wait for the cauliflower to cook, it usually takes 8 minutes (Okay it's more than five minutes, but you can dance to this in the meantime
  6. Take out the cauliflower from the water and serve it in a bowl
  7. Enjoy by dipping the pieces of cauliflower directly in the hummus, huuumm
